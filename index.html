GETTING STARTED

Redactr-App is a web app that could help you safely share content on social media by scrambling portions of texts you wouldn't want the world to see/read

Mode of Operation

1. Write/paste the content into the specified form field

2. Specify the words you need to be scrambled into the specified input field in the form filed. Note: more than one word are separed by space

3. Specify what to use as replacement for the words that you want to scramble. For dashes (-), underscores (_), example asterisks (*), question marks (?), or even other words.

4. Click on the "Redactr Now" button to redact your content
